Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing plans

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll get immediate access to new features. When you downgrade, changes will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
What happens if I don't find a match?
We're confident in our matching system, but if you don't find a suitable match within 6 months of premium membership, we'll extend your subscription for another 3 months at no additional cost.
Are there any hidden fees?
No, there are no hidden fees. The price you see is exactly what you'll pay. All features listed in your plan are included without any additional charges.
How secure are the payment transactions?
All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels using industry-standard SSL technology. We partner with trusted payment gateways to ensure your financial information is completely secure.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied within the first week, contact our support team for a full refund.
